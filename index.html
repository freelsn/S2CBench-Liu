The Main Heading

S2CBench stands for Synthesizable SystemC Benchmark suite. It is a open source SystemC benchmarks created to help designers evaluate the QoR of state of the art HLS Tools. All of the main HLS tools support the synthesizable subset of SystemC and hence should be able to synthesize all of these designs without any modifications.

Most of the work has been done by the DARClab at the Department of Electrical (ECE), at the University of Texas at Dallas.
